Summary

"Bahubali" is an epic Indian film directed by S.S. Rajamouli. The movie follows the story of two brothers, Amarendra Baahubali and Bhallaladeva, who belong to the ancient Mahishmati kingdom. The plot revolves around their rivalry for the throne and the struggles faced by their mother, Sivagami, who is the queen of Mahishmati. The story begins with the birth of Amarendra Baahubali, who is prophesied to be the savior of the kingdom. However, due to political intrigue and betrayal, he is separated from his family and grows up as a commoner named Shivudu. Meanwhile, his brother Bhallaladeva ascends to the throne with the help of his cunning advisor, Bijjaladeva.
